Dataset Construction of Multi-round Dialog

We aimed to build a large dateset (about 20,000,000 pieces) of multi-round dialog, which is essential for training a chatbot.

The dataset is crawled from daily conversations in weibo, which is a popular social media in China. Due to its strong anti-crawler machanism, ongoing work is focus on how to guarantee a stable crawler program.

Named Entity Recognition System

In this project, we seek for fast and accurate tagging system aiming to find out three kinds of phrases (i.e., named entities): person name, location and organizarion. Also, our system differs from others in that our target is to tag sentences on daily dialogs.

A survey on this topic is firstly performed in order to figure out the path researchers have taken. Then we built a strong baseline system using a graphical model called CRF. Future plans involved testing on more casual dialogs.
